Programs

St. Jude's mission is to remove every tech barrier between a sick child and a cure.

  • Clinical IT: keeps patients alive with connected care systems.
  • Research IT: accelerates discovery through genomics, AI, and data sharing.
  • Business IT: supports donors, cybersecurity, and hospital operations.

This program transforms IT from a cost center into a force multiplier. Better data systems produce faster cures and strengthen public trust.
